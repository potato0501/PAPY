The greatest food in the world!

spaghetti!!!

September 20, 2015 papyrus

How to cook a perfect pasta:
- A perfect dry pasta
- A can of pure tomato sauce
- A pinch of raw spices, such as oregano or dried parsley
- Delicious water
Method:
- Throw the dry pasta into a bowl, without salt or oil, just plain.
- Add cold water, just enough to cover the pasta.
- Microwave it at maximum power for 8-10 minutes!!!
- When it's done, add a generous amount of tomato sauce without draining!!!
- Stir it a little, like giving a friend a massage.
- Sprinkle a little "professional soul spice" on it at the end.
